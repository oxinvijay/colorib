22 Best Mobile-First Free & Premium AMP Themes 2024

 July 27, 2024  Julia Starlark  Theme Collections  0 Comments

Here is a collection of the best free and premium AMP themes to improve and skyrocket websites’

mobile functionality.


A website that does not load correctly and quickly does not have the brightest future ahead of it.


It makes visitors leave early, impacting your business’s overall performance.

You are losing potential clients, but your bounce rate is also being affected,

which triggers search engines. (Your page won’t be Google-approved.)


There are many ways to improve loading speeds and make your site lightning fast. One of

the best options is the AMP Project (Accelerated Mobile Pages).

What Is the AMP Project?




It is an open-source initiative focusing on boosting a website’s mobile performance

(launched by Google and OpenJS Foundation). The future of the internet is mobile, meaning every website should

be working fast and flawlessly on any device.


The AMP Project has these special tweaks in the HTML code, making for a lightweight website.

Since the product came from the search engine behemoth Google, you can also see an increase in

the mobile-friendly test for all the websites powered by AMP.


Post navigation Skip to WordPress themes Skip to HTML templates However, if you already use a super-fast theme, you might not need to upgrade it with

the AMP Project. Chances are, you will not see any drastic improvements. But if you are starting,

you should look into it.


Pick the best AMP theme and speed up your website!

1. Divi


Divi is the very newbie-friendly answer to all your website ideas. Regardless of the project

you would like to start with, you can make a page for it without a hassle.


It will take you barely any time to get things moving forward. Why? Divi has unlimited

possibilities and options for every single one of you.


Keep in mind that there are 800+ ready-to-use designs available for you to

put into play out of the box.


You will also find Divi builder, a drag-and-drop page-building technique that allows you to

be as creative as you want to be.


You can modify everything!


Divi is flexible, mobile-friendly, and responsive for a smooth operation.




2. Jevelin



If you are searching for a comprehensive tool to help you establish multiple websites,

you came to the right place.


Here’s Jevelin, a striking web design for anyone looking for a quick website fix.

Once you unbox the special Jevelin package, you can expect an immediate realization of

your new website.


It is no secret that Jevelin is a full-blown solution for building websites for agencies,

freelancers, businesses, bloggers, and online store owners.


However, the possibilities go over and above once you implement the power of WPBakery

page builder.


You can edit the layout and modify Jevelin to your needs and wants without changing the code.

Jevelin’s responsiveness is top-notch, and it can instantly be reshaped for

any device.




3. Jawn


Jawn is an AMP theme for online magazines and newspapers. This marvelous

web design has several demos so you can quickly find the best look.


You can also edit Jawn’s look and create a dream page for all your compelling

news and articles.


However, you will surely dig Jawn’s out-of-the-box look due to its minimalistic and

clean approach. Thus, Jawn easily caters to different niches and works well for creating a

general news website.


Some goodies include auto-post load, email subscription, outstanding loading speed,

search engine optimization, unlimited colors and more.


Sticky navigation, footer reveal, and back-to-top buttons are just a few

additional Jawn specialties.




4. MagPlus


MagPlus is a modern and lightweight WordPress blog and magazine website theme.

It is an intuitive framework to put together awesome fresh websites.


MagPlus makes design and customization an effortless visual process.

You don’t need to write a coding line to get professional results.


Let WPBakery do the heavy lifting, with 40 demo websites and 150 custom elements.

MagPlus has sleek Bootstrap coding to provide ample cross-compatibility with

devices and browsers.


Mobile users make up a sizable slice of every market around. MagPlus provides AMP-ready

functionality for your articles and posts.


Let MagPlus expand your audience and let you reach a broader crowd.




5. Pearl


Pearl is a multi-purpose, all-in-one AMP theme with a solution for nearly any business.

Numerous exclusively designed demos are at your service, so building something from

scratch is unnecessary.


These are perfect for corporate, factory, construction, medical, and restaurant websites.

Note: You can easily modify each demo however you want.


Pearl has amazing performance and responsiveness, 200+ custom modules,

and a batch of plugins.


With an awesome slider, handy mega menu, drag-and-drop header builder, and tons of advanced

theme options, you do not have to be a coder to create a page with Pearl!




In Conclusion to the Top Gadget WordPress Themes

You can’t help but check the list of premium gadget WordPress themes above. Each option matches

the best web design standards to ensure you get the best. Getting your website

live is no longer rocket science, no matter how skilled you are. Your task is to take the time to pick

the right option for you. And you’d better not make it in a hurry.
