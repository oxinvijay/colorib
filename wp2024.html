Any business requires building connections with clients and other brands. An up-to-date and functional website will help you communicate with a wide audience, especially now in 2022.


In this collection, we have assembled the best newsletter WordPress themes for your business that will help you manage the continuous flow of news and share it with your clients and followers regularly. This is all thanks to the automated newsletter dispatch that MailChimp plugins provide.


You can be sure of the quality of this email marketing tool, as there are over 20 million customers out there that use this tool to build connections.


Check out the best newsletter WordPress themes that will help you run efficient email marketing campaigns in 2022.



Best Newsletter WordPress Themes for Email List Building


Qwery – Multi-Purpose Business WordPress Theme + RTL


You would need a great WordPress theme if you want to achieve maximum success for your website,

and Qwery would be just the thing for you today. Built in-line with the best web design standards,

Qwery is a multipurpose option worth trying. You can use it for multiple modern websites that promote a

new product or service. Along with all that, Qwery supports the WooCommerce plugin.

It is also compatible with the Booked Appointments & LatePoint Booking plugins. The best way to start with

this theme is through the pre-designed page layouts and growing from there. Customize every tiny detail of

your website as per your liking.



Jacqueline | Spa & Massage Salon Beauty WordPress Theme + Elementor



If you are a sports content maker who wants to refresh a website design in 2022 and make

your interaction with the audience more effective, go for the FC United theme. This one is among the best

sports newsletter WordPress themes that will help you establish an automated newsletter dispatch of

the latest sports news and events within your website.


The FC United theme features lots of sports content that you can use to launch a trendy and

relevant website without much effort. Another amazing tool that helps with content management is the Elementor

Page Builder, which allows you to change the website look and fill it with custom content without

touching a single line of code. With this theme, you can have fun when creating a website while enjoying

the content management process.



Briny | Scuba Diving School & Water Sports WordPress Theme + RTL


Briny is one of the best WordPress newsletter themes for the creative online representation of any project.

It is especially good for websites dedicated to sports as it was originally

made for scuba diving and water sports enthusiasts.


This theme has a bright and trendy design that will catch the attention of every one of your visitors.

Like most of the best newsletter WordPress themes for business, Briny was made using a drag-and-drop page builder.

Another advantage is the integration of a clever appointment management system that allows

the organization of schedules right through the website.


Briny can be used for commercial projects, magazines, and blogs. It is fully compatible with

the WooCommerce plugin so that you can build a full-fledged e-store with it. In addition, for bloggers,

there are lots of blog features, advanced blog settings, and a variety of premade blog

layouts ready for use.



Ekoterra – Social Activism, NonProfit & Ecology WordPress Theme


Ekoterra is a robust, responsive, and stylish Petition and Wildlife Protection WordPress theme with multiple uses.

This theme is built on the powerful ThemeREX framework and offers four different design styles.

It’s constructed using Elementor, a versatile WordPress page builder. Ekoterra comes with a

range of shortcodes and widgets, is ready for SEO, and offers full petition functionality through SpeakOut!

Email Petitions for online petition signing. It also includes the Give – Donation plugin for fundraising,

MailChimp for WP, The Events Calendar, etc. In addition to its many widgets, shortcodes, and flexible

theme options, Ekoterra provides numerous pre-made pages, making it easy for you to set up your website without spending

too much time creating content.



Good Energy – Ecology & Renewable Power Company WordPress Theme


Good Energy is a fresh and stylish Ecology WordPress theme with multiple uses. It’s designed for

eco-related businesses, and you can also use it to promote eco-friendly movements, environmental protection,

natural power sources like solar and wind, or simply create a blog about saving our Mother Nature.

It’s also great for eco-volunteering. This theme comes with a bundle of useful plugins:

WPBakery (formerly known as Visual Composer, which we believe is better than Elementor), Essential Grid

Plugin for eco galleries, and the ThemeREX Donations plugin for fundraising. Additionally, GOOD Energy is GDPR ready, ensuring

that it complies with data privacy regulations.



In Conclusion to the Top Gadget WordPress Themes

You can’t help but check the list of premium gadget WordPress themes above. Each option matches

the best web design standards to ensure you get the best. Getting your website

live is no longer rocket science, no matter how skilled you are. Your task is to take the time to pick

the right option for you. And you’d better not make it in a hurry.
