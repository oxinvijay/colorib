15 Best Lookbook WordPress Themes for Fashion and Creative Projects

 July 27, 2024  Julia Starlark  Theme Collections  0 Comments

The first impression always matters. It’s especially true when we are talking about fashion and style.

The visual appeal and striking content presentation on such websites play major importance. When you

start a blog, portfolio, or online store related to the fashion or creative industry, you need to

focus on its style and remarkable content presentation. You need to focus on many details

when building such websites. Designing and developing every element of such a web page from scratch can be a

time-consuming and exhausting process. However, things get so much easier when you use the best lookbook WordPress themes.


If you are going to launch a fashion, style, or another creative web project, consider using

the premium WordPress themes from the collection below. When you use a ready-made design,

you have more time to focus on the content and other vital aspects of your website.

The following lookbook WordPress themes let you take full control over your website, and manage its visual appeal and functionality just

the way you like.

Using the Qwery WordPress theme, you can launch a competitive and stylish website for

your personal or corporate project. It’s a multi-concept WordPress theme, which is loaded with an

impressive collection of 100+ stunning homepage demos. The fully responsive theme’s layout includes

everything needed for launching a striking and competitive website. It’s made with the Elementor page

builder and doesn’t require any coding skills to be modified yourself. The Qwery theme is integrated with a

bunch of handy WordPress plugins that can significantly improve your website’s usability and make it more functional.

Two online booking options are included in the theme’s pack and are available for free use.

Qwery seamlessly integrates with WooCommerce and includes many inner pages and layout styles,

which you can use on your website out of the box.



Jacqueline | Spa & Massage Salon Beauty WordPress Theme + Elementor



Podium is a striking WordPress theme perfectly suited for a modeling agency, model’s portfolio,

modeling school, catalog of top models, fashion magazine, fashion boutique, and other fashion-related websites.

It’s a beautiful and stylish WordPress theme featuring a fully responsive and Retina-ready layout.

The theme is equipped with a series of premium plugins. WPBakery Page Builder lets you customize

the theme code-free. Using the Revolution Slider, you can update your website’s front page with a

captivating slider that can easily adjust to all screen types. The theme’s compatibility with the Events Calendar

and MailChimp should help you keep the audience better informed about your upcoming

events and recent news.



Briny | Scuba Diving School & Water Sports WordPress Theme + RTL


Ingenioso WordPress theme features a fresh and stylish design. It’s the ultimate choice for

building contemporary marketing studios, creative agencies, web designers’ portfolios,

marketing bureaus, and other topic-specific projects. The theme should be useful for building online and

offline portfolios. It includes a collection of 5 creative homepage demos and a wide choice of inners

pages and layout styles that you can edit according to your company’s needs. The built-in custom color

scheme editor lets you fully control your website’s colors. The theme is ready to be used for building blogs

and online portfolios. It contains creative agency and personal portfolio layouts

ready to be imported to your website.


Briny can be used for commercial projects, magazines, and blogs. It is fully compatible with

the WooCommerce plugin so that you can build a full-fledged e-store with it. In addition, for bloggers,

there are lots of blog features, advanced blog settings, and a variety of premade blog

layouts ready for use.



Ekoterra – Social Activism, NonProfit & Ecology WordPress Theme


Lione WordPress theme features a fresh and trendy design, which can easily adjust to all devices

and web platforms. The theme includes 8 premade homepage demos and 7 beautiful portfolio styles. You may

choose any design that you think will fit your project the most. The theme is best suited for launchin

g a creative agency or advertising company website. Thanks to its integration with the Elementor

page builder, you can take full control over the theme’s look and feel code-free. Also, it seamlessly

integrates with such popular WordPress plugins as Slider Revolution and WooCommerce. You may choose any free or

premium plugin from third parties and install it on your website easily. Besides, you can use the theme for

blogging purposes. It includes modern blog styles and galleries, which you can integrate into

your website.



Good Energy – Ecology & Renewable Power Company WordPress Theme


Helion is a great multi-concept WordPress theme. It includes a collection of 11 stunning

homepage demos, which you can import to your website with a click and adjust according to your specific

requirements code-free. Besides being made for fashion and other creative needs, the theme fits cryptocurrency

and NFT web projects. It includes an NFT portfolio homepage that you can use for launching your topic-specific

web resource. The theme is made with the Elementor page builder and includes a collection of useful

shortcodes and widgets that you can use to edit your website’s content. The theme also includes ready-made

designs and elements suited to showcase your skills, job experience and achievements, missions, and priorities,

provide testimonials, and showcase your portfolio on one page.



In Conclusion to the Top Gadget WordPress Themes

You can’t help but check the list of premium gadget WordPress themes above. Each option matches

the best web design standards to ensure you get the best. Getting your website
