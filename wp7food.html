7 Best Seafood WordPress Themes For Restaurants 2024

 July 27, 2024  Julia Starlark  Theme Collections  0 Comments

Fresh seafood is a pure temptation that makes millions around the globe get that urge to order sushi

for lunch or travel to another part of the world just to try a freshly-caught lobster. As the demand for

seafood is so high, the niche grows fast, too. As a result, seafood restaurants are mushrooming at

the speed of light.


Launch a website if you are running your seafood restaurant and you feel like your business has

reached a kind of development plateau wherein you need a tool that would boost your business efficiency.

A website is the best way to showcase products and services to a wide audience. This is why we have prepared a

list of the best seafood WordPress themes with beneficial features for

your business.


In the list below, you will find many responsive WordPress themes packed with an online

reservation system, drag-and-drop content composer, social media widgets,

and delicious content.


No more procrastinating. Time to dig into the collection of best seafood WordPress

themes in 2023.

Best WordPress Themes for Seafood Restaurants


1. Seafood Company & Restaurant WordPress Theme


Let’s start the collection of the best seafood WordPress themes with this beautifully designed

contemporary WordPress theme. This one has many options suitable for a seafood company or establishment.

This theme has an absolutely responsive design, so all the content you share will look perfect on

the visitor’s preferred device. It has several ready-to-use demos, so you can save your time and pick one

that seems like the best pick for your particular project. The Recipe Management option is a lovely option

that makes this theme different from many other seafood WordPress themes.


It is good to know that the theme is WooCommerce-integrated, so you won’t face any difficulties

launching an eCommerce website. A bunch of ready-made pages is already included in the package.

With this theme, you can showcase your products elegantly and effectively.


Here is a list of other plugins compatible with this theme: Slider Revolution, Swiper Slider,

Essential Grid, Gutenberg, WPBakery Page Builder, WPML, and the GDPR Framework WP. Due to the General

Data Protection Regulations compliance, you can legally process data of EU citizens,

thus expanding the area of your influence.


The theme has a reservation system and an integrated event management plugin, which means you can

take bookings right from your website. This theme has features, yet the design is welcoming and

simple enough to adapt to any restaurant, cafe, or bar.



2. Asia Garden | Asian Food Restaurant WordPress Theme



Most Asian dishes are centered on seafood. Therefore, we would highly recommend to all

those entrepreneurs that are desiring to bring their Asian restaurants to the Internet space to look for

the best seafood WordPress themes. Such an approach will narrow your search and help you find the theme

that best suits your business. Asian Garden is a customizable WordPress theme. You can use both plugins and

theme options admin panel to add changes to your website design and fill it with unique content. Moreover,

you will get access to a huge pack of shortcodes, flexible color palette, and a wide range of Google Fonts.


Asia Garden is a theme that can be used as a restaurant visit card website and an online

food delivery service. Apart from the table reservation system, the theme supports the Contact Form 7 plugin

, allowing your customers to contact you easily.


With this theme, you can show off the beauty of Asian cuisine by launching a blog and sharing

delicious recipes with a wide audience. In that case, the advanced blog settings will come in handy.

A blog will not only entertain your visitors, but it will also improve

the SEO of the whole website.



3. Fish House | A Stylish Seafood Restaurant / Cafe / Bar WordPress Theme


Fish House is a modern and engaging theme that can arouse interest even across the pickiest audiences.

This theme is a great choice if you want to create a fancy foodie-oriented website.

The theme has advanced booking forms, shortcodes, and ready-made modules (products /team/ locations/etc.).

Like other premium seafood WordPress themes, it has beautiful blog skins. It offers a wide

range of blog options if you want to share recipes, tell more about your upcoming events, announce new arrivals,

and promote seasonal dishes.


Of course, you will be able to manage your content and craft pages all by yourself.

This is because of the drag-and-drop builder which requires no coding experience whatsoever.

The theme has a beautiful header that you can also change using various header styles

that come with this theme.


The very important feature of this theme is its responsiveness. There is no chance for a

non-responsive website to become competitive and generate traffic and sales. This is why taking care of

the website’s look on mobile devices is so important. With the Fish House theme, you don’t even need to

think about it since the theme adapts itself to any device.



4. Giardino | An Italian Restaurant & Cafe WordPress Theme


Giardino is a functional and authentic WordPress theme coded with high-quality code.

It has a gorgeous slider which makes a homepage even more spectacular. Use it to tell your brand story or

introduce new menu pieces. By the way, the theme design is fully-responsive and sliders are mobile-ready.


This is one of those seafood WordPress themes that requires minimum knowledge for website

customization since it is packed with the WPBakery page builder and has a theme options admin panel.

Apart from a comprehensive interface, you get it compatible with the following plugins: Booked,

Essential Grid, Slider Revolution, Instagram Feed, ThemeREX add-on, and more.


Let us speak more about the ThemeREX add-on, as it is a plugin that is worth the attention.

Thanks to this plugin, you can modify the header and footer, and use custom post types. Of course,

the main purpose of all seafood WordPress themes is to demonstrate beautiful dishes to visitors, and

the Giardino theme has restaurant menu management, which allows you to do it with ease. You can use premade galleries, events calendars,

and table schematics. Giardino is the theme that is advantageous for both

entrepreneurs and customers.



5. Nigiri – A Modern Restaurant WordPress Theme


Are you looking for a restaurant website that would fit your sushi restaurant? You should look

no further, as the Nigiri theme is a great option to stop for. It has a clean minimalist design that is fully

responsive. You will get many ready-made inner pages and layout variations for your shop and blog. With

the WooCommerce plugin, selling culinary equipment and ingredients online becomes possible. Each of the ready-made pages of this website is highly customizable. You can adjust the theme look

according to your needs with the drag-and-drop functionality the WPBakery Page Builder provides.

What’s amazing is that you can get the theme right away using the One-Click installation option. Nigiri includes a wide range of shortcodes, all suitable for restaurant businesses. Moreover,

it features lots of Google Fonts and a customizable color scheme. The theme is great for international

websites, as it supports the WPML plugin. This plugin allows the translation of the content

into various languages. Other features of this theme are a powerful admin panel, social media widgets, custom post formats

(audio, quote, standard, video, gallery, link), various header styles, customizable Google Map, boxed and

fullwidth layout options, smooth scroll, video backgrounds, etc.



In Conclusion to the Top Gadget WordPress Themes

You can’t help but check the list of premium gadget WordPress themes above. Each option matches

the best web design standards to ensure you get the best. Getting your website

live is no longer rocket science, no matter how skilled you are. Your task is to take the time to pick

the right option for you. And you’d better not make it in a hurry.
