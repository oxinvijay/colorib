47 Olympic Logos and Symbols From 1924 to 2028

Last week, I visited my friend in China and couldn’t help but notice Paris 2024 symbols everywhere.

This made me look into the history of Olympic logos; the oldest I could find was dated back to 1924.


I noticed that Sochi’s first logo features domain extensions (Sochi.ru). We will most likely see similar trends in the

upcoming years. I was surprised that some logos use flat designs, which became popular only a few years ago.

Olympics logos must be kept clean and easy to remember; each country has done a fantastic job.


Another standout Olympic logo is certainly LA 2028, a mix of classic and modern fonts mixed in one.
